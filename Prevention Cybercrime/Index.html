Welcome to Stop Cybercrime

This website is dedicated to raising awareness and providing information on cyber crime and how to prevent it. Cyber crime is any illegal activity that involves a computer, a network, or a device. Cyber crime can affect individuals, businesses, and governments in various ways, such as identity theft, fraud, hacking, phishing, ransomware, cyber bullying, cyber stalking, cyber terrorism, and more.

Cyber crime is a serious threat to our society and security. According to the [Cybersecurity Ventures], cyber crime will cost the world $10.5 trillion annually by 2025. That is more than the combined GDP of China and India. Cyber crime also poses a risk to our personal data, privacy, and online safety.

On this website, you can learn more about cyber crime and how to stop it. You can also contact us, report cyber incidents, and support organizations that fight against cyber crime. We hope you find this website useful and informative.
