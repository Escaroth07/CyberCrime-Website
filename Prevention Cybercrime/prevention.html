Prevention Tips

Stay safe online with these tips:

  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly.
  • Use antivirus software and keep it updated.
  • Use a firewall and a VPN to secure your network and encrypt your online traffic.
  • Be careful of what you click or download. Do not open suspicious links or attachments.
  • Be wary of phishing emails or messages that ask for your personal or financial information.
  • Backup your data regularly and store it in a safe place.
  • Educate yourself and others about cyber crime and its consequences.
  • Support organizations and initiatives that fight against cyber crime and promote cyber security awareness.
